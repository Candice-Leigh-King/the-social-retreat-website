Pre-coursework

The Social Media Retreat isn’t open for just any type of digital creator! We like our students to meet a standard before arriving at our retreats that’s why we are going to have you prepared and ready to go with our pre-course work. We’ll build your mindset, grow your brand, and create your engaged community so you are ready to land paid brand deals by the start of this social media bootcamp.

Introduction

Welcome! We’ll talk about all the future success that is heading your way and share the overview of your journey through the pre-course leading up to the start of the retreat.

Personal Branding

In this section we're going to dive deep into the mindset it takes to be a successful brand. We'll teach you how to brand yourself, do's and dont's, work through your dreams, values and purpose. Then we'll show you the best techniques to make your brand unique and recognizable. You'll have a fun branding activity to complete too.

Finding your Target Audience

e will walk you through what kind of person your brand represents so you can attach a unique style, niche, and personality to your brand. Then we’ll discover exactly who your ideal follower, audience, and customer is and turn them into a figurative person that will guide your business decisions in all your future content.

Growth & Engagement

Before we can start to collaborate with brands we need to have an engaged community. In this section we’ll walk you through the fundamental methods to gain new followers and the best techniques to keep them engaged so that you can have a successful paid collaboration in the future.

Collab Activity

Finally! Your first taste of what we’ll be covering during this retreat! In this section we’ll show you exactly how to find the perfect brands to work with so you are ready to start pitching when you arrive in Kenya!

Summary

We’ll briefly summarize our pre-course work and make sure you are exactly where you need to be before we start the social media bootcamp so that you can have the best chance at success in your future collaboration opportunities on the retreat.

Bonus lesson

We’ll give you some extra tips and tidbits, and show you some of our favorite apps that you can start using right away to make yourself a better creator.
