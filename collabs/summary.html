Summary

Essence

Your essence is the central brand theme. Narrow down your entire brand and vision into one word.

Purpose

Finding your purpose gives you greater meaning and direction. Your purpose is your essence in action.

Values

Define your main value and sub-values. Doing this helps you understand who you are and the kind of community you want to be part of.

Niche

You have two target audiences.

1. Primary Target Audience.

This is your community
Don't be affraid to niche. The great niche is a mix of your 1 favorite interest and your 1 favorite skill.

1. Secondary Target Audience.

The brands you want to work with
These are the brand that inline with your values and want to get in front of your community.
